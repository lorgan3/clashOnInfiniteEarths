PC controls

Moving
Click the screen to trigger a pointer lock and give this website permission to use it.
  • With pointerlock: Simply move the mouse left and right to turn in that direction.
  • Without pointerlock: Move your mouse to the side of the window that you want to turn to.
Selecting abilities
Either scroll with the mousewheel or press 1, 2 or 3 on the keyboard to select the ability.
Using abilities
Right click to use the current ability. Each ability has a cooldown, but you can use multiple abilities at once.
  • #1 Laserbeam: Fires a laser out of your eyes that splits asteroids in two. It can also stun enemy players for a short time.
  • #2 Punch: A classic punch that minifies asteroids and knocks out enemy players.
  • #3 Speed boost: Quickly accelerate to advoid incoming attacks or to get coser to your target.

Mobile controls

Moving
  • On modern devices you can rotate the device to move in that direction.
  • Slide your finger over the screen to turn on other devices.
Selecting abilities
There are two possibilities, depending on the size of your device.
  • Click an ability in the HUD to select and use it on smaller devices.
  • Click the left side of the screen to select the next ability on bigger devices.
Using abilities
Click the right side of the screen to use the currently selected ability.

Tips & tricks

Singleplayer
  • Destroy all big asteroids to win the game. Their current count is indicated on your HUD.
  • Consider using your fists instead of your laser since they have a shorter cooldown and cause less debris.
  • Try punching asteroids from the side to prevent the debris from hitting you.
  • Use your speedboost to advoid collisions or to catch up with fast moving asteroids.
Multiplayer
  • Kill all enemy players to win the game. Their current count is indicated on your HUD.
  • Track enemies through the world by looking for their red reticles or by following their smoke trails.
  • Try stunning your enemies to prevent them from getting away.
